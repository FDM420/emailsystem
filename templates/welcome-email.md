## Use it in Outlook / Gmail / iPhone

If you want to read your business email inside Outlook, Apple Mail, or the Gmail app on your phone, use these server settings:

**Incoming Mail (IMAP) — recommended:**
- Server: `tuesday.mxrouting.net`
- Port: `993`
- Security: `SSL/TLS`
- Username: your full email address
- Password: your mailbox password

**Outgoing Mail (SMTP):**
- Server: `tuesday.mxrouting.net`
- Port: `465`
- Security: `SSL/TLS`
- Username: your full email address
- Password: your mailbox password

**Tip:** If port 465 is blocked by your internet provider, try port 587 (STARTTLS) or 2525 (STARTTLS) instead — same server, same username/password.
